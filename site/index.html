ABOUT US

We are a DJ collective with over two decades of combined industry experience.
So what makes us different?

Distinctive Style

Every DJ loves music but we can deliver it for you in a way that is unique and creative. Our passion to blend all genres of music seamlessly gives us the edge to relate with wide audiences throughout different generations, and create memories that you will treasure.

Our Tools

We are collective of DJ’s that remain authentic to our vinyl roots, delivering music digitally across 4 turntables. Now add that to the Intelligent Lighting, Visuals, DJ Booths, Dancefloors, Backdrops, Up-lighting and any other extras you’d like, we can execute any scale of event.

Hosts

Not all DJs are cut out for public speaking. We host events with energy and charisma ensuring that you and your guests soak up the atmosphere whether it be a corporate affair, wedding celebration, bar or club night. Expect a clear, concise delivery to help manage your guests.

Tailored to you

We believe every client deserves a different approach and the key to a successful event demands a customized solution. We don’t believe in set packages. No matter the size of the venue or the type of occasion, we commit to working with you to provide a bespoke package.
